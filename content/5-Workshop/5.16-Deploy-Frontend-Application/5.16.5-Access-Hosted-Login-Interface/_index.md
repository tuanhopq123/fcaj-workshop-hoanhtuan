#### Step 1: Open the Production URL

1. Click on the production branch URL generated under your AWS Amplify Hosting console.
2. The browser will navigate to your public web address hosting the **AWS Serverless Project**.

#### Step 2: Verify Login Page Render

1. Verify that the **Đăng nhập (Sign in)** interface for the **Smart Home Energy Waste Monitoring** system renders cleanly.
2. Ensure the login card correctly prompts for **Email** and **Password** inputs securely linked with Amazon Cognito authentication.
