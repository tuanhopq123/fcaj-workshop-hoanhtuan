AWS Lambda is used to process telemetry data, detect energy waste, store results in DynamoDB, and publish alerts to SNS.

1. **Search for Lambda**
   * In the AWS Console search bar, type `Lambda`.
   * Select the **Lambda** service.

2. **Open Functions**
   * In the left menu, click **Functions**.

3. **Start creating a function**
   * Click **Create function**.
