date: 2026-07-07
weight: 10
---

Deleting the DynamoDB table will wipe out all underlying project data categories, including Rooms, Telemetry, Alerts, and Reports Metadata. This action is irreversible.

1. Search for **DynamoDB** and access the control dashboard.
2. From the left navigation menu, select **Tables**.
3. Check the box next to the target table: `EnergyWasteData`.
4. Click the **Delete** action button.

![Select DynamoDB Table](/images/5-Workshop/5.18-CleanUp/Picture9.png)

5. Type `confirm` into the validation field and click **Delete table**.

![DynamoDB Tables Cleared](/images/5-Workshop/5.18-CleanUp/Picture10.png)
