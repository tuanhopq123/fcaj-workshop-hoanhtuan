date: 2026-07-07

---

When you delete an SNS Topic, any subscriptions belonging to that Topic are automatically removed as well. There is no need to clear email subscriptions separately beforehand.

1. Search for **SNS** and open **Amazon Simple Notification Service**.
2. In the left navigation menu, select **Topics**.
3. Select the topic: `energy-waste-alert-topic`.
4. Click the **Delete** button.

![Select SNS Topic](/images/5-Workshop/5.18-CleanUp/Picture3.png)

5. Type `delete me` into the confirmation field and click **Delete**.

![SNS Topic Cleared](/images/5-Workshop/5.18-CleanUp/Picture4.png)
