Amazon SNS (Simple Notification Service) is used to send email alerts when energy waste is detected.

1. **Search for SNS**
   * In the AWS Console search bar, type `SNS`.
   * Select the **Simple Notification Service**.

2. **Open Topics**
   * In the left menu, click **Topics**.

3. **Start creating a topic**
   * Click **Create topic**.
