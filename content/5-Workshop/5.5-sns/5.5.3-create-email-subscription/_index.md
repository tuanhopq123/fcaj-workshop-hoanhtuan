1. **Open Subscriptions**
   * On the `energy-waste-alert-topic` page, scroll down to the **Subscriptions** section.
   * Click **Create subscription**.

2. **Configure the subscription**
   * Topic ARN: leave as the `energy-waste-alert-topic` topic.
   * Protocol: **Email**.
   * Endpoint: your email address.
