#### Overview

In this section, we will configure **AWS WAF (Web Application Firewall)** to protect our application hosted on AWS Amplify. By integrating WAF, we add a crucial layer of security, safeguarding the dashboard against common web vulnerabilities, malicious bots, and unauthorized traffic. We will walk through the firewall setup, rule configuration, and verification of protection logs to ensure the dashboard remains secure.
