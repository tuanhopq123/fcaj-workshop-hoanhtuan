#### Execution Steps

1. Find and open the **AWS Amplify** service.
2. Select your application: `energy-waste-dashboard`.
3. In the left-hand menu, look for: **Hosting** -> **Firewall**.
4. Click the **Add firewall** button.

![Open Amplify Firewall](/images/5-Workshop/5.17-cloudfront-waf/5.17.1-amplify-firewall.png)

#### Enable AWS WAF for Amplify

Follow the interface prompts to enable AWS WAF protection for your application.

![Enable AWS WAF](/images/5-Workshop/5.17-cloudfront-waf/5.17.1-enable-waf.png)
