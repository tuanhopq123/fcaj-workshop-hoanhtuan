1. In the AWS Console search bar, type: `EventBridge`.
2. Choose the service: **Amazon EventBridge**.
3. In the left menu, find the group: **Scheduler**.
4. Choose: **Schedules**.
5. On the Schedules page, click: **Create schedule**.
