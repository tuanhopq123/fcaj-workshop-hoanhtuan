This step is mandatory to prevent the system from continuously creating telemetry, logs, and emails[cite: 12].

1. Go back to: **Amazon EventBridge → Scheduler → Schedules**[cite: 12].
2. Choose the schedule: `schedule_virtual_sensor_every_1_min`[cite: 12].
3. Click: **Disable** (or go to Edit -> Disable schedule -> Save)[cite: 12].
4. Verify the status is now **Disabled**[cite: 12].
