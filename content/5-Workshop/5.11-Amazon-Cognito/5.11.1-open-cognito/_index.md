1. Check the top right corner: **Asia Pacific (Singapore) ap-southeast-1**.
2. In the AWS Console search bar, type: `Cognito`.
3. Choose: **Amazon Cognito**.
4. In the left menu, choose: **User pools**.
5. Click: **Create user pool**.
